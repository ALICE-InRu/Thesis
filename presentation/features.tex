    \pause\item [\PhiSet{LPT}] the SDR longest processing time (LPT).
    \pause\item [\PhiSet{LWR}] the SDR least work remaining (LWR).
    \pause\item [\PhiSet{MWR}] the SDR most work remaining (MWR).
